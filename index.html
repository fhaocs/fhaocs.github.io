Fei Hao, Ph.D.


Associate Professor


School of Computer Science
Shaanxi Normal University

Email: fhao@snnu.edu.cn

Address: 620 West Chang'an Avenue, Xi'an, 710119 China

I received the B.Sc. degree in Information and Computing Science and the M.Sc. degree in Computer Software and Theory from Xihua University, China, in 2005 and 2008, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Soonchunhyang University, South Korea, in 2016. Since 2016, he has been with Shaanxi Normal University, Xi'an, China, where he is an Associate Professor. He is currently taking a Marie Sklodowska-Curie Individual Fellowship at the University of Exeter, Exeter, United Kingdom.

My research interests rotate around the broad aspects of social computing, ubiquitous computing, graph neural network, big data analysis and processing and mobile cloud computing. I'm looking for motivated students. If you are interested in doing research with me, please feel free to contact me via email.
